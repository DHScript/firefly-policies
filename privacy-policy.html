Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 17, 2025

DappGames ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Firefly Connect mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you use Firefly Connect, we automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (IDFA with your consent)
  • Usage Data: Gameplay statistics, levels completed, scores, time spent in app
  • Performance Data: App crashes, loading times, error logs for debugging purposes
  • Approximate Location: Coarse location data for ad targeting (with your consent)

1.2 Information You Provide

  • Purchase History: Records of in-app purchases and subscriptions managed by Apple/Google
  • Settings & Preferences: Audio settings, haptic feedback preferences, game progress

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  • App Functionality: Save game progress, track achievements, manage purchases
  • Personalized Advertising: Show relevant ads through Unity Ads (requires ATT consent)
  • Analytics: Improve gameplay experience, fix bugs, optimize performance
  • Subscription Management: Validate VIP subscriptions, manage benefits

3. Third-Party Services

Firefly Connect integrates the following third-party services:

3.1 Unity Ads

We use Unity Ads to display advertisements. Unity Ads may collect:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA) with your consent via App Tracking Transparency (ATT)
  • Device information and coarse location for ad personalization
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

Unity Ads Privacy Policy

3.2 Apple In-App Purchases

All purchases are processed through Apple's App Store or Google Play Store. We do not store your payment information. Apple's and Google's privacy policies apply.

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

4. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

Before collecting your IDFA for personalized advertising, we will request your permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. You can:

  • Allow Tracking: Receive personalized ads based on your interests
  • Ask App Not to Track: Receive non-personalized ads

Your choice does not affect core app functionality. You can change this setting anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

5. Data Storage and Security

Game data is stored locally on your device using secure methods:

  • Game progress saved in device storage (PlayerPrefs) with integrity validation
  • No data transmitted to external servers except through third-party services listed above
  • Purchase receipts validated through Apple's/Google's secure servers

6. Children's Privacy

Firefly Connect is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Control Ad Tracking: Manage ATT consent in iOS Settings
  • Manage Subscriptions: Via Apple App Store or Google Play subscriptions settings
  • Delete Data: Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Opt-Out of Analytics: Enable "Limit Ad Tracking" in iOS Settings (iOS 14.4 and below)

8. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary:

  • Game progress: Until you uninstall the app
  • Analytics data: Aggregated for up to 24 months
  • Crash reports: 12 months for debugging purposes

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or this page. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. Unity Ads operates globally and may transfer data to the United States and other countries. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.
